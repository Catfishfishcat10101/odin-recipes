Crispy Hash Browns

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, shredded
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear, then drain and squeeze dry.
  3. Place shreds in a bowl and mix in the onion, flour, and egg until evenly distributed
  4. Heat about 1/4 inch of oil in a large heavy skillet, place potatoes in pan.
  5. Cook until nicely browned on bottom. 5 minutes per side.
  6. Remove and drain
  7. Serve hot!
